Statistical Analysis of Health and Stress Data.

The Project's intent is that your stress factor inventory, along with all who complete this questionaire contribute to a better understanding of the role of stress in the development illness and disability.

This current application is focused on the preliminary data gathering phase by presentation of a well known Life Style Assessment Tool for completion by our site visitors. Minimal demographic information: email (for follow-up and to check for multiple submissions by the same individuals), the first 3 digits of your zip code (which identifies only a US region), which age range you fall into and finally whether you agree to be contacted for follow-up of you subsequent state of health. Everything else is a group of multiple choice questions.

Any data collected, is only saved anounymously, with your agreement, and is subject only to use by this project in a pooled (non-individually identifiable) form. No individual information is shared outside of the project and is stored elsewhere, off-line.

We hope you will consider contibuting to this very important study of the stress on Americans and the potential prediction of risk of subsequently developing illness.

How to Use the Application

Respondant-User

If you are taking the survey or intend to, this part is for you. If we have done our jobs well, the survey should be intuitive for you and we should have reassured you that your personal data, as limited as it is is not accessible to anyone but those in the study protocol authorized to deal with it.

  1. Go to the Survey page.
  2. Enter the brief age group, regional and follow-up (email) information as asked.
  3. Complete the Survey itself.
  4. Pressing the submit button Encrypts and Stores your responses.
    The responses are stored separately from the geographic information and email, They can only be merged later, offline by the study analysts.
  5. Your illness risk for the subsequent 2 years will be returned to you at the bottom of the page.
    The displayed information is not retained and will be lost when you leave the page
  6. In 2 years a very brief follow-up questionaire will be sent to you at the email you supplied for you to complete.(This is absolutely the only use made of the email information)
    The email questionaire will just ask for your current state of health. No identifying information is asked at that time.
  7. Complete the follow-up questionare at that time and hit the return button. This will allow us to check the relationship of your risk score with any change in your health.

The data obtained is studied as part of an aggregate (summary) of data and no scores are used individually. Only the means (average) and variation (standard deviation) of all the data together are used for the study. This may be brokendown for analysis by age group or reqions, the very at most.
